# ArcKit Test Project: Scottish Courts and Tribunals Service

This is an ArcKit test repository for exploring GenAI strategy for the Scottish Courts and Tribunals Service (SCTS).

## About SCTS

The Scottish Courts and Tribunals Service supports justice by providing the people, buildings and services needed to support the judiciary, the courts, devolved tribunals and the Office of the Public Guardian.

**Courts and Tribunals:**
- Supreme Courts (Court of Session, High Court of Justiciary)
- Sheriff Appeal Court (civil and criminal)
- Sheriff Courts and Justice of the Peace Courts
- Scottish Tribunals
- Office of the Public Guardian

**Digital Services:**
- Civil Online platform
- Case Tracker
- Livestreaming of appeals
- Judgment search and download

## Project Focus

GenAI strategy for modernising court services and improving access to justice.

## Getting Started

Use ArcKit slash commands to generate architecture artifacts:
- `/arckit.stakeholders` - Analyze stakeholder drivers
- `/arckit.requirements` - Generate requirements
- `/arckit.research` - Research GenAI solutions
- `/arckit.ai-playbook` - Assess AI Playbook compliance
